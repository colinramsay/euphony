euphony by Colin Ramsay

The most lightweight audio player for OSX

Euphony is an audio player with minimal UI and a need for speed.

Upon launch, Euphony sits in your menu bar and allows you to select a directory of songs for playback. Global hotkeys let you pause and skip tracks, and Growl notifications tell you what track is playing.

Euphony does not integrate with iTunes, it does not have a library, it does not have theming or scripting support - because there's nothing to theme or script! It's responsive and it plays music. What more do you need?
